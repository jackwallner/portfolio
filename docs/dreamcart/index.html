A virtual fashion shopping game

DreamCart

Browse, build your cart, check out with coins, and style your dream closet. The dopamine hit with zero buyer's remorse.

Window shop without the bill.

DreamCart turns the satisfying parts of shopping into a playful iPhone game. Browse real-looking fashion, fill your cart, unlock outfits, and collect pieces for your virtual closet. Everything in DreamCart is imaginary. No real merchandise is sold or shipped.

Every dayStart with free coins

Claim your daily drop, keep your streak alive, and spin the wheel for bonus coins.

BrowseBuild the cart

Mix tops, bottoms, shoes, jackets, jewelry, and accessories into looks you actually want to save.

CollectGrow your closet

Check out with virtual coins, unbox your finds, and use the outfit builder to make a look of your own.

Virtual only: DreamCart items and coins have no cash value. Optional coin packs are for in-app use only, and DreamCart does not sell or ship physical products.
