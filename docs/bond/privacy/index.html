Bond Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2026

Overview

Bond is a couples app for iPhone and Apple Watch. It helps partners stay connected through love-language reminders, shared milestones, daily check-ins, and relationship insights. Your privacy and the privacy of your relationship are fundamental to how Bond is built.

Data We Collect

Bond collects only the data needed for the app to work as a shared couples experience.

  • Account: Sign in with Apple provides an anonymous user identifier; you may choose to share an email via Apple’s private relay
  • Profile: display name and love-language preferences you enter
  • Reminders & milestones: content you and your partner create
  • Check-ins: daily question responses you submit (visible to your partner after you both answer)
  • Device tokens: for push notifications when you enable them
  • Location: only when you create location-based reminders — used on-device and for geofence triggers, not sold or used for ads

Bond does not sell your data. We do not run third-party advertising or behavioral analytics in the app.

How Data Is Stored

Bond uses Supabase (encrypted Postgres on AWS) for account, pairing, reminders, milestones, and check-in sync between you and your partner. Data is encrypted in transit (TLS) and at rest. See Supabase security.

Authentication & Pairing

Sign in with Apple handles login; Bond never receives your Apple ID password. Invite links pair two accounts so you can share reminders and milestones. Either partner can unpair at any time.

Push Notifications

Notifications (reminders, milestones, pairing) are delivered via Apple Push Notification service. You can turn them off in iOS Settings → Notifications → Bond.

Purchases & Subscriptions (Bond+)

Bond+ is an optional subscription processed entirely by Apple StoreKit. Bond never receives or stores payment card details, billing address, or Apple ID credentials.

RevenueCat helps verify subscription status so Bond+ features unlock on your device. RevenueCat receives purchase receipts from Apple, not your full payment profile. See RevenueCat Privacy.

Subscription management (cancel, refund requests) is handled in your Apple ID account settings.

Third-Party Services

  • Apple — Sign in with Apple, push notifications, in-app purchases
  • Supabase — backend database and auth
  • RevenueCat — subscription entitlement checks

Data Retention & Deletion

Your data is kept while your account exists. To delete your account and associated data, use in-app account controls or email bond@jackwallner.com. We will delete your profile, reminders, milestones, and pairing records within 30 days of a verified request.

Children’s Privacy

Bond is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their information.
