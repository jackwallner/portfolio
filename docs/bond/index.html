The pitch

Most “couples apps” feel like another social feed. Bond is narrower: help two people remember to act on love languages, celebrate milestones, and check in once a day.

The free tier covers core reminders and milestones. Bond+ is for couples who want daily check-ins, insights, smart location reminders, and template packs.

Bond+

Optional auto-renewable subscription via the App Store, with a free trial when eligible. Unlock Daily Check-In, love-language insights, smart & location reminders, and curated templates. Manage or cancel anytime in Apple subscription settings.
