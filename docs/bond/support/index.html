Getting Started

  • Sign in with Apple — one tap, no password to remember.
  • Share your invite link from the Pairing screen; your partner taps it to connect.
  • Create reminders, log milestones, and try Daily Check-In when you have Bond+.

Bond+ Subscriptions

  • Purchases and free trials are handled by Apple. Use Restore purchases on the paywall or in Settings if premium features don’t unlock right away.
  • Manage or cancel anytime: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions, or Bond Settings → Manage subscription.
  • Refunds are requested through Apple; Bond cannot refund App Store charges directly.

Troubleshooting

  • Partner can’t see reminders: Confirm you’re both signed in and still paired.
  • No push notifications: Enable notifications for Bond in iOS Settings.
  • Pairing link didn’t work: Generate a new invite from Pairing and try again.
  • Stale data: Sign out and sign back in to refresh your session.
  • Location reminders: Allow location access (including background if you want geofences while the app is closed).

Privacy

Bond uses Sign in with Apple and Supabase for sync. No ads, no third-party analytics. Read the Privacy Policy.
