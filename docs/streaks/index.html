Streak Finder

App Store

Mines 400 days of Apple Health to surface streaks across 9 metrics. Ranks by interestingness, nudges at 7 PM when the hero streak is at risk.

App Store ↗

The pitch

You already have streaks — you just don't know it. That 47-day walk streak, the 12-week sleep average above 7 hours, the 3-month daily stand goal. They're buried in HealthKit, invisible.

Streak Finder surfaces them automatically, ranks them by how impressive they actually are, and warns you before you break the good ones.

Specs

Platform iOS 17+, watchOS 10+
Language Swift 6 with strict concurrency checking
Data SwiftData cache, App Group shared with widgets
Metrics Steps, exercise, stand, energy, distance, flights, workouts, sleep, mindfulness
Analysis 400 days of history, daily + weekly cadences, threshold tiers
Notifications 7 PM at-risk nudge for hero streak

What mattered

The discovery engine. Every metric has 6 threshold tiers (e.g., 3k, 5k, 7.5k, 10k, 12.5k, 15k steps). The engine evaluates every `(metric, cadence, threshold)` combo across 400 days, finds contiguous streaks, and filters for minimum viability (3+ days daily, 2+ weeks weekly). Then it ranks by a weighted score: streak length × threshold difficulty.

Hero streak + badges. One streak gets featured — your best current run. Everything else appears as tappable badges. The hero rotates automatically as streaks break and new ones form. No manual curation, no decision fatigue.

Calendar heatmaps. Each streak has a year-view heatmap showing every hit and miss. Green intensity maps to how far above threshold. It's visceral — you can see your life patterns: the sick week, the vacation, the consistent stretches.

The 7 PM nudge. If your hero streak isn't "locked in" for the day (current day has no data yet), you get a gentle notification at 7 PM. Not nagging — context. You decide if you care enough to save it.

Widget reliability. All widgets read from a snapshot JSON in App Group UserDefaults — never HealthKit directly. The main app refreshes this on every foreground and background task. Widgets are instant, not laggy.

You don't build streaks. You discover you already had them.
